❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about FreshMart

🛒 Ordering

Browse our product catalog, add items to your cart using the quantity selector, then click "Proceed to Checkout." You'll need an account to complete your purchase. Payment is processed securely on the checkout page.
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 30 minutes of placement. After that, the order enters processing and changes may not be possible. Contact our support team immediately if you need to make changes.
There is no minimum order amount. However, free shipping is available on orders over $35. Orders under $35 may incur a small delivery fee depending on your location.
Open your cart sidebar and look for the "Enter coupon code" field. Type in your code and click "Apply." Valid codes include SAVE10 (10% off $20+), FLAT5 ($5 off $30+), and WELCOME15 (15% off any order). Discounts are shown in the pricing breakdown immediately.

🚚 Delivery

We deliver 7 days a week from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2:00 PM. Weekend deliveries are available in most metro areas.
Standard delivery takes 1-2 business days. Same-day delivery is available in select areas for orders placed before 2:00 PM. Express 2-hour delivery is available in major cities for a small fee.
We currently serve most major metro areas across the US. Enter your zip code during checkout to confirm delivery availability at your address. We are continuously expanding our delivery zones.
For most deliveries, our driver will leave the package in a safe location at your door. For orders requiring a signature (such as alcohol), a second delivery attempt will be made the next day. You'll receive a notification with delivery updates.

💳 Payment

We accept Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Digital Wallets (PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay), Bank Transfers, and Cash on Delivery. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all transactions. We never store your full card number — only the last 4 digits are kept for your reference. Our payment processing is PCI DSS compliant.
For card and wallet payments, you are charged immediately when you place your order. For bank transfers, payment is due within 24 hours of placing the order. Cash on Delivery is paid to the delivery driver at time of delivery.

↩️ Returns & Refunds

We accept returns on non-perishable items within 7 days of delivery in original, unopened packaging. For perishable items (fresh produce, dairy, meat), we offer exchanges or refunds if the product is defective or damaged on arrival. Contact us within 24 hours of receiving the order.
To request a refund, go to your Order History in My Profile, find the order, and click "Request Refund." Alternatively, contact our support team via the Contact page with your order ID and reason. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
We sincerely apologize! Please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with a photo of the issue and your order number. We will arrange a replacement delivery or full refund, whichever you prefer, at no additional cost.

👤 Account

Click "Sign In" in the top right corner, then select "Sign Up." Fill in your name, email, and a password (minimum 6 characters). Your account is created instantly and you can start shopping immediately.
Click "Sign In" and then "Forgot password?" Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires in 24 hours. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
Sign in to your account, then go to My Profile → Order History tab. You'll see all your orders with status, date, payment method, and itemized details. Click any order to expand and see the full breakdown.
You can save any product to your wishlist by clicking the heart (🤍) button on any product card. Sign in is required. Access your wishlist from My Profile → Wishlist tab, where you can add items to your cart or remove them from the wishlist.
