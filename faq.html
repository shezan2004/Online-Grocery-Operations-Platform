Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order? +

To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in first. Once you've filled in your delivery address and selected your payment method, your order will be confirmed.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept multiple payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Digital Wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal)
  • Bank Transfers
  • Cash on Delivery
How long does delivery take? +

Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days depending on your location. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

Is there a minimum order amount? +

No, there's no minimum order amount. However, free shipping is available on orders $50 and above. For orders under $50, a flat shipping fee of $4.99 will apply.

Can I return or exchange items? +

Yes! We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Products must be unused and in original packaging. Perishable items have a 3-day policy. Contact our customer service for return instructions.

How do I use a discount code? +

You can apply discount codes on the checkout page. Popular codes include:

  • WELCOME - 5% off first order
  • SAVE10 - 10% off orders $50+
  • SAVE20 - 20% off orders $100+
How do I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package in real-time. You can also view your order status in your profile under "Order History".

Is my personal information safe? +

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and payment information. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I contact customer support? +

You can reach our customer support team through:

  • 📧 Email: support@supermart.com
  • 📞 Phone: 1-800-SUPERMART (24/7)
  • 💬 Live Chat: Available on our website
  • 📝 Contact Form: Visit our contact page
Do you offer a loyalty program? +

Yes! Our SuperMart Rewards program gives you points on every purchase. Earn 1 point per dollar spent and redeem 100 points for $10 off. Members also get exclusive deals and birthday discounts!
